Strategic Challenges & Solutions

Transforming raw market volatility into structured business intelligence.

The Problem

Data Fragmentation

Historical price data for multiple assets (BTC, ETH, BNB) exists in disparate, uncleaned formats with inconsistent date schemas.

Volatility Measurement

Stakeholders lack a clear visualization of intra-day risk (High-Low spreads) and market dominance shifts.

Macro Analysis Barrier

Difficult to correlate individual coin performance with total market capital growth over multi-year cycles.

The Solution

Python-Driven ETL

Implemented a robust EDA pipeline to normalize date formats, handle missing values, and calculate technical rolling averages before ingestion.

DAX Financial Measures

Developed custom DAX formulas to dynamically calculate Market Cap Dominance % and Moving Averages for trend confirmation.

Multi-Tier Dashboard

Architected a 3-page reporting system providing Executive, Tactical, and Macro views for comprehensive decision making.

Python EDA
Deep-Dive Analysis

Prior to visual development in Power BI, a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis was performed using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn). This ensured data quality and uncovered hidden correlations.

Correlation Matrix

Uncovered high price correlation (0.95+) between major assets during market surges.

Outlier Detection

Identified and smoothed black-swan trading volume spikes to prevent skewed visuals.

Automated Cleaning

Python script standardized 10,000+ rows of Binance/Bitcoin history into a unified schema.
